About Us

We provide virtual assistants that are trained to handle prospects according to your business needs. That includes and is not limited to Real Estate & Solar lead generation, acquisitions, lead closing, generating sales & general assistance ex. Rental Management.

In SRC we focus on your ROI and how to actually be a supporting function to your overall operations by giving you outsourced callers with indistinguishable accents and knowledge that allows them to function perfectly in your local/external market.

Our Services

Tailored solutions for your business needs

Residential Cold Calling

Virtual agents that are specialized in lead generation and prospecting residents with intent to sell their properties. Suited for companies and individuals looking for outbound lead generation and off market opportunities.

Acquisitions

We provide closers that are qualified to follow up on your leads and take it to the finish line resulting in an increase in your Contracts/Listings/Product Sales.

Commercial Cold Calling

Trained personnel that possess knowledge when it comes to the commercial side. Key merits our agents have are holding up conversations with business owners and extracting needed information that would help push the lead further.
